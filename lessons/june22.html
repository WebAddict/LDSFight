Reach Out With Love

When a young man is tasked with inviting his neighbor and peer to church, he learns it takes more than one try to have success. In this light-hearted take on a challenge many face, we are reminded to never stop inviting others to accept the gospel of Jesus Christ. Sometimes we may wonder, “What is the point? Will my friend ever be interested?” While it will not always be easy, it will never be impossible. What can you do to invite those you know and love to learn more about the gospel?
