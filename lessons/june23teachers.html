Teacher's Lesson: Guided Safely Home

In 1939, the new battleship Bismarck launched from Hamburg, Germany. It was the most powerful vessel on the water and was considered unsinkable. But when a torpedo jammed its rudder, the Bismark lost its ability to steer to safety, away from the enemy. We live in a world full of conflict. Like that battleship, we will sink without a sense of direction. Jesus Christ provides that direction, and living His teachings will steer us safely home.

What will you do to reach up and obtain heavenly help today?
