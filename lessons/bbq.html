Does claiming Rewards take away from my F.I.G.H.T. points?

NO! Your SPENT points is kept track of seperately. You are encouraged to spend your hard earned points!

Can I do old lessons that I missed, or only new lessons?

YES! You can go back at any time and complete all of them! Just getting started? Just start with two-three lessons a day to get caught up!

Does going to 2nd Sunday Sundaes count towards Duty to God?

YES! If you spend 10+ mins on Duty to God, you can count it!

I don't have a Journal...

There are so many ways to RECORD your feelings. A Journal doesn't have to be a book, but it should be something that can last for a long time and be something you read/review in a much later time of your life or pass on to your children and grandchildren.

Here are some ideas for an alternates to a "Book" style Journal:
Email yourself! Write an email, just address it to yourself.
Word Document or similar digital document saved to your computer
Video / Audio Recordings - save them to a journal folder
App! - there are hundreds of Journal/Note Apps
LDS Account - Under 'My Account > Notes > Journal' - It is saved to your LDS account and will last FOREVER! *My personal favorite
Gospel Library App - Under 'Notes > Journal' - It is saved to your LDS account and will last FOREVER! *My personal favorite

Can I invite other's on the LDS Fight app?

YES! Anyone can join the app! However only Deacons, Teachers and Priests from our Allen Ranch Ward can claim prizes and post things and leave comments.
