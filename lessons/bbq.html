Frequently Asked Questions

How many points are there?

There are over 30,000 total points before the BONUS and hidden points - In theory, there could be well over 40K points...

How hard is it to get to 5K (5,000 points)?

In only TWO Weeks, you could have:
1,400 Scripture points
about 2,000 Lesson points
over 300 Fight Challenge points
700 Journal points
500 Duty to God points
150 Scouting points
1,000 Temple points
200 Testimony points
-----------------
6,250 Points in 14 days

Don't forget there is also 3,000 MEMORIZE Points! You will also be awarded 1,000 points per non-member friend that you bring to church (3hr block)! There are service points, classroom participation... There are so many ways to hit 5k!

Can I do old lessons that I missed, or only new lessons?

YES! You can go back at any time and complete all of them! Just getting started? Just start with two-three lessons a day to get caught up!

Does going to Mutal count towards Scouting?

YES! If you spend 10+ mins on scouting, you can count it!

Does going to 2nd Sunday Sundaes count towards Duty to God?

YES! If you spend 10+ mins on Duty to God, you can count it!

I don't have a Journal...

There are so many ways to RECORD your feelings. A Journal doesn't have to be a book, but it should be something that can last for a long time and be something you read/review in a much later time of your life or pass on to your children and grandchildren.

Here are some ideas for an alternates to a "Book" style Journal:
Email yourself! Write an email, just address it to yourself.
Word Document or similar digital document saved to your computer
Video / Audio Recordings - save them to a journal folder
App! - there are hundreds of Journal/Note Apps
Gospel Library App - There is a Notes > Journal area - and it is saved to your LDS account and will last FOREVER!

Can I invite other's on the LDS Fight app?

YES! Anyone can join the app! However only our Deacons, Teachers and Priests from our Allen Ranch Ward can claim prizes and post things and leave comments.
