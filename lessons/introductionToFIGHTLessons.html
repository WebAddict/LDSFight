Whether or not we realize it, we ARE fighting spiritual battles every day. We are constantly making choices every moment with what we do with our time. Each choice can have a positive or negative impact on us. The F.I.G.H.T. program is designed to help us strengthen our spirit and testimonies and to put more trust in our Heavenly Father and his plan.

Daily F.I.G.H.T. Lesson Schedule

Sundays: Bishop's Message

Mondays: General Conference Talk

Tuesdays: Christ Attribute

Wednesdays: Duty to God

Thursdays: Quorum Message

Fridays: Missionary

Saturdays: Service

Points:

1 pt per day: Scripture Reading for 10+ mins

1 pt per day: Complete the daily F.I.G.H.T. Lesson

3 pts per Assigned Scripture and Song Memorization (10 total memorizations)

5 pts per Temple Visit (up to 5 visits per month)

5 pts per Attend Mission Prep

5 pts per Attend 2nd Sunday Sundaes

5 pts per Share Testimony From Popet

5 pts per Teach Gospel principles, splits with Missionaries

Points tallied on LDSFight.com

Rewards:

Each Young Man will have the opportunity to earn points and can use them to claim rewards. Rewards will be evolving through the 2 month program. The YM with the most points will receive the Gilbert Temple Picture (donated by Bro Jaime Johnson)
